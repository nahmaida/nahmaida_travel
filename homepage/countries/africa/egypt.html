Egypt

Egypt is known for its rich ancient history and landmarks such as the Pyramids of Giza, Majestic ancient structures near Cairo, a testament to Egypt's architectural prowess and rich history, including the iconic Great Pyramid and the Sphinx. It has a diverse culture influenced by both African and Middle Eastern traditions.

Sharm El Sheikh

A popular resort town on the Red Sea, Sharm El Sheikh is renowned for its stunning coral reefs, vibrant marine life, luxurious resorts, and exciting water sports.

Suez Canal

An engineering marvel, the Suez Canal is a crucial waterway connecting the Mediterranean Sea to the Red Sea, facilitating global maritime trade.

Sinai Mountains

This rugged mountain range is steeped in biblical history, offering breathtaking landscapes, trekking opportunities, and the historic Mount Sinai, where Moses is believed to have received the Ten Commandments.

Cairo

Egypt's rather ugly capital, Cairo is a city of contrasts, blending ancient wonders like the Pyramids of Giza with modern urban life, vibrant markets, and rich cultural heritage.

Copts

The Copts are Egypt's Christian minority, with a history dating back to the earliest days of Christianity. They have a unique cultural and religious heritage, contributing significantly to Egypt's diversity.
