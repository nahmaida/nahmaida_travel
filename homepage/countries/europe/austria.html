Austria

Austria is known for its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and vibrant cultural scenes. From stunning parks to grand palaces, Austria has much to offer.

Vienna

Vienna, Austria's capital, is known for its artistic and intellectual legacy shaped by residents including Mozart, Beethoven, and Sigmund Freud.

Schonbrunn Palace

Schonbrunn Palace, a former imperial summer residence, is one of the most important architectural, cultural, and historical monuments in Austria.

Hundertwasserhaus

Hundertwasserhaus, an apartment house in Vienna, is famous for its unique and colorful design by artist Friedensreich Hundertwasser.

Streets of Vienna

The streets of Vienna are lined with grand buildings, cozy cafes, and vibrant street life, reflecting the city's rich cultural heritage and the serious, imperial atmosphere.

Stephansdom

St. Stephen's Cathedral, or Stephansdom, is the mother church of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Vienna and the seat of the Archbishop of Vienna.

Belvedere Palace

Belvedere Palace is a historic building complex in Vienna, consisting of two Baroque palaces, the Orangery, and the Palace Stables.
