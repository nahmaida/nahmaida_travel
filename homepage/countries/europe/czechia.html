Czechia

Czechia is a country with a rich history and beautiful architecture, known for its markets, historical cities, and cultural heritage.

Prague

Prague, the capital of Czechia, is known for its Old Town Square, colorful baroque buildings, Gothic churches, and the medieval Astronomical Clock.

Charles Bridge

The Charles Bridge is a historic bridge that crosses the Vltava river in Prague, adorned with statues of saints and offering stunning views of the city.

Vaclav Square

Vaclav Square is one of the main city squares and the center of the business and cultural communities in the New Town of Prague.

Streets of Prague

The streets of Prague are filled with charming architecture, historic sites, and a vibrant atmosphere that captures the essence of the city's rich culture.

Prague Castle

Prague Castle, a historic fortress and the official office of the President of the Czech Republic, offers a stunning view of the city and the Vltava river.

Astronomical Clock & Old Town Square

The Astronomical Clock in Old Town Square is one of Prague's most famous landmarks, known for its intricate design and hourly show.
