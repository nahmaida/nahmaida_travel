France

France is celebrated for its rich cultural history and landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, showcasing its architectural prowess and artistic heritage. The country's diverse culture is influenced by both European and global traditions.

Paris

Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Iconic landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum.

The Louvre

The Louvre Museum is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris. It is home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

The Eiffel Tower, a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world, is an iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris.
